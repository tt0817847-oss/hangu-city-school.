App Description:
Empowering Parents, Connecting Teachers, and Simplifying School Management.
Welcome to the official digital portal of The Hangu City School. Designed specifically for our parents and staff, this app brings the entire school experience to your fingertips—from Playgroup to Class 10.
Key Features:
📊 Real-Time Attendance: Stay updated! Receive instant notifications the moment your child is marked absent. Track monthly attendance history with a single tap.
💰 Online Fee Management: No more queues. View pending dues, pay fees online, and generate digital receipts instantly.
📚 Daily Homework & Notifications: Get daily homework updates and official school announcements directly on your dashboard.
💬 Direct Teacher Chat: Have a question? Use the built-in chat box to communicate directly with class teachers for academic updates and feedback.
🏫 Complete Class Coverage: Specialized management for all levels—from our youngest learners in Playgroup & Nursery to our seniors in Class 10.
📄 Google Sheets Integration: Teachers can update student records via Google Sheets, ensuring the most accurate and up-to-date data is always available.
Why Choose Our App?
We believe in transparency and modern education. This app ensures that parents are always part of their child's academic journey, making school management seamless, paperless, and efficient.
